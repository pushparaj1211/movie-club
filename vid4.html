naruto

Naruto is a popular anime show that aired on TV Tokyo for five years, from 2002 to 2007. The series follows the journey of a young boy named Naruto Uzumaki, who dreams of becoming Hokage,the leader of his village of Konohagakure. However, he faces many obstacles along the way, including the rejection of his fellow villagers due to a dangerous fox spirit that dwells within him. The show is known for its vibrant cast of characters, each with their own unique storyline which eventually intertwines with Naruto.
